# 🌙 Drift

> A tiny browser-based bedtime game set in space. Float through the dark, meet a small alien, discover hidden activities. Sleep science integrated in quietly, with no timers, no scores, just drift.

## ✦ What is this

Drift is a slow, dark, interactive experience built for the space between awake and asleep.

You are a tiny astronaut. You float. Things happen quietly around you. There is no objective, no score, no timer, no way to lose. Just open it at night, lights off, and let it run.

---

## ✦ Features

- A little astronaut who reads, naps, blows bubbles, stretches, and journals
- Bioluminescent blobs that breathe on a hidden 4-7-8 rhythm
- A tiny alien who visits sometimes and slowly drifts away
- A focus star that brightens and fades to anchor wandering thoughts
- Everything dims gradually over 25 minutes
- A rocket cursor that lazily floats through space
- Hidden easter eggs to stumble upon

---

## ✦ Hidden sleep science

Nothing is labelled. Nothing instructs you. But quietly underneath:

| What you see | What it's doing |
|---|---|
| The glowing blobs expanding and contracting | Pacing your breath to a 4-7-8 rhythm |
| Clicking to nudge the astronaut — resistance, then release | Progressive muscle relaxation |
| The alien's body glowing slowly from feet to head | A guided body scan meditation |
| One star that brightens, holds, then fades | Single-point focus to catch a wandering mind |
| The whole scene dimming over 25 minutes | Gradual light reduction to cue sleep onset |

## ✦ Easter eggs

There are a few things to discover. No spoilers — but here are some hints:

- The astronaut notices when you go very still
- The focus star is tappable
- Three slow taps anywhere does something
- Leave it alone long enough and the astronaut makes their own decision
- The alien appreciates company

## ✦ How to run it

It's a single `.html` file. No dependencies, no build step, no npm install.

**Option 1 — just open it:**
```
download drift.html → double click → opens in browser
```

**Option 2 — host it free on Netlify:**
1. Go to [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
2. Drag and drop `drift.html`
3. Done — live link in seconds

**Option 3 — clone and run locally:**
```bash
git clone https://github.com/diyanosauras/drift.git
cd drift
open drift.html
```
